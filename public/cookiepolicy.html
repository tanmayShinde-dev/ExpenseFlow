Learn how ExpenseFlow uses cookies to improve your experience and keep your data secure.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help websites remember information about your visit.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Improve site performance
  • Analyze usage patterns

3. Types of Cookies

  • Functional: Save your preferences
  • Analytics: Help improve the platform

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use trusted third-party services for analytics and performance monitoring.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features.

This Cookie Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page.
